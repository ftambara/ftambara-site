Signal Processing

This will be an exploration of ideas in signal processing. Along the way, I will make many mistakes and reinvent the wheel. That is by design. The purpose is to have a fun project as an excuse to investigate concepts which could include linear algebra, assembly, web, fourier transforms, signal processing itself, among others.

The initial project will be an application of Nyquist's theorem of minimal sampling rate. To make things easier, I will work with a single channel on a one-dimensional signal.

I think I lack deep knowledge in most of the areas I'm about to dive in. I hope to look back in a few weeks and observe that to be no longer the case. Let's have fun.
